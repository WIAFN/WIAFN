Was It All For Nothing?

WIAFN?

“Was it All For Nothing?” is a video game based on first person shooter, a genre that mainly focuses on precise mouse movements and high reflex responses, and rogue-lite, a genre that focuses on generally increased difficulty and rich replayability, genres. While there are very successful rogue-lite and first person shooter games available, no game has managed to capture the essence of two very popular genres together. Was it All for Nothing aims to be the game that acts like a bridge between the two genres, bringing the best of both into one.
